K.K's App

A simple Mac app for converting images, audio, and video — plus scanning documents. No installs, no fuss.

Download for Mac

Version 1.0.2

Requires macOS Sonoma (14) or later.

First launch: Since this app isn't from the App Store, macOS may ask you to approve it. Right-click the app and choose Open, then click Open again in the dialog. You only need to do this once.
